On September 7, 2023,
have a tooth crowned with gold.

On October 6, 2023, 
have another tooth crowned with silver.
Discovered an unerupted wisdom tooth 
in the upper jaw through X-ray.
I once had 
a recurring dream 
during middle school.
In the dream, 
I was wearing 
my school uniform 
and holding a fetus, 
whose origin I didn't know. 
I threw it into the trash can 
and went into the classroom. 
Throughout the class, 
I sat pale, sweating profusely. 
As soon as the bell rang for break time, 
I dashed to the trash can. 
Holding the recovered fetus, 
I felt an inexplicable sense of fulfillment.
